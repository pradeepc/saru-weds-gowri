About Us

Saravana Dhanpal,

Entertainer & Performing Artist
 

Namaste, My name is Saravana Dhanapal. I was born in Bangalore during February of 1985 and I've been performing on stage since the age of 7. I founded Agnii - Art For All, an organisation that was incorporated in the year 2000 as an entertainment group which performed Fire Dances, Drum Circles and Larger than life Puppet Shows. I've had the honour of taking the flavour of Indian performing arts to places like Sweden, France, Italy, England, Spain, Germany, U.A.E, U.S.A, Japan, Pakistan, Nepal, Austria and many more. These and many more experiences through the span of my career have molded me in to who I am today.  As of now, I like to dabble my feet as a unique entertainer, puppet artist and also an actor.
